Import contacts

Copies contacts from Leads Database email_contacts (default: Norway) into the contact sheet. Skips contacts already in the sheet.

Push selected to CRM

Reads the contact sheet, filters rows where Select is filled in, groups by site, pushes new sites to the CRM template sheet.

Sync CRM to Leads Database

Syncs the CRM template sheet to Leads Database crm/crm_template. Pushes crm_status, crm_sales_person and crm_date back to site_leads. Run after manually updating Status or Selger in the template.

Workflow
  1. Import contacts — pulls contacts from the Leads Database into the contact sheet. Choose country and size filters to control quality.
  2. Review & select — open the contact sheet, review the list and fill the Select column for contacts you want to pursue.
  3. Push selected to CRM — takes all selected contacts, groups by site, and adds them as new rows in the CRM template.
  4. Work the CRM — fill in Status and Selger in the CRM template as you progress with each lead.
  5. Sync CRM to Leads Database — pushes status and salesperson back to the Leads Database so reporting stays up to date.
