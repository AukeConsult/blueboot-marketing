CRM Discover

This is the manual discovery route — you import a selection of contacts from the leads database into a spreadsheet, review and mark the ones worth pursuing, then push them into the CRM work sheet for deeper qualification. For a fully automated alternative, use Filter facets to build a campaign directly from the contact pool.

1

Export contacts

Pull contacts from Leads Database into the contact sheet. Choose country and size filters.

2

Review & select

Open the contact sheet, review the list and fill the Select column for contacts you want to pursue. If you also fill in a campaign name in the Campaign column, you can use the campaign route instead — go to Campaigns and click Discover campaigns to create the campaign and import the contacts automatically.

Contact sheet
3

Push selected to CRM

Takes all selected contacts, groups by site, and adds them as new rows in the CRM work sheet.

4

Work the CRM

Fill in Status and Selger in the CRM work sheet as you progress with each lead.

CRM work sheet
5

Sync CRM to Leads Database

Pushes crm_status, crm_sales_person and crm_date back to the Leads Database. Run after updating Status or Selger in the template.
